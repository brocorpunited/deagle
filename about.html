About Double Eagle

Double Eagle Firearms & Pawn, or Double Eagle for short, is a family owned and operated local business. First establishe in 2008 and located in the historic Bryant Hardware building in downtown Arkansas City, KS.

Offering a clean, family friendly environment, Double Eagle provides:

  • Fast, Easy Cash loans
  • Good Quality Used Merchandise
  • New and Used Firearms
  • Firearm Accessories

Double Eagle is also a licensed Class 3 dealer. We are happy to sell or transfer your NFA items, as well as assist you in filing out the necessary paperwork

Come visit Double Eagle Firearms & Pawn! We add new inventory daily, so you never know what you will find. We have a helpful, friendly staff, a large selection, and some of the best prices in town.
